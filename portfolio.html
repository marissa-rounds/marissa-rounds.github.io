Industry Issue TED Talk

This presentation addresses the growing crisis in family law, where financial barriers, court backlogs, and inconsistent policies limit access to justice. My strategy focused on combining research with real-world insight from a practicing attorney to highlight both systemic issues and practical solutions, such as expanding mediation and increasing legal aid access. This issue matters because delays and inaccessibility directly impact families and children, often prolonging unsafe or unstable situations and undermining the fairness and effectiveness of the legal system.
