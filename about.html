About Me

Professional Biography

Marissa Rounds is an undergraduate student at The University of Tampa pursuing a double major in Political Science and Communication and Speech Studies, with a strong interest in law and public policy. She brings experience in legal and administrative settings, having interned at Newburgh City Court and currently serving as a Student Assistant Coordinator in the Office of Residence Life. Marissa is known for her strong communication skills, attention to detail, and ability to manage high-volume, confidential work with accuracy and professionalism. In addition to her professional experience, she serves as President of Delta Phi Epsilon, where she leads a large team and oversees organizational operations. Marissa plans to attend law school and pursue a career in the legal field, with a focus on advocacy, policy, and impactful leadership.

Core Strengths

  • Professional written and verbal communication
  • Leadership and team coordination
  • Legal and administrative support experience
  • Policy and advocacy interest
  • Organization, accuracy, and confidentiality
