Leadership

President, Delta Phi Epsilon

As President of Delta Phi Epsilon, I lead a chapter of diverse members by overseeing operations, guiding executive board initiatives, and fostering a strong, values-driven community. I work closely with campus partners, advisors, and national leadership to ensure organizational success while promoting academic achievement, philanthropy, and personal growth. This role has strengthened my leadership, communication, and decision-making skills while allowing me to create meaningful experiences that empower members and leave a lasting impact on our campus.

First-Year Studies Mentor

As a First-Year Studies Mentor, I support incoming freshmen and transfer students as they transition to college life and adjust to a new academic and social environment. I provide guidance on campus resources, time management, and student involvement while fostering a welcoming and supportive space for questions and growth. Through one-on-one mentoring and group engagement, I help students build confidence, develop independence, and successfully navigate their first year at the University of Tampa.

Brotherhood Chair, Phi Alpha Delta Law Fraternity

As Brotherhood Chair for Phi Alpha Delta, I focused on strengthening member relationships and cultivating a sense of community within the organization. I planned and executed social events and engagement initiatives designed to build trust, connection, and collaboration among members. Through this role, I developed skills in event planning, teamwork, and member engagement, while helping create an inclusive and supportive environment that enhanced the overall fraternity experience.
