Banned Works Paper

This artifact is a research paper analyzing North Korea’s ban on blue jeans as a symbol of political oppression and restricted personal freedoms. I created it to examine how seemingly small cultural restrictions reflect broader systems of control, using constitutional comparisons and credible sources. This work demonstrates my ability to conduct in-depth research, apply critical thinking, and connect global issues to legal principles, particularly the importance of First Amendment rights and the protection of individual expression.

Evidence Evaluation Paper

This artifact is an evidence evaluation paper analyzing research on wrongful convictions and the effectiveness of state-level investigative reforms. I created it to critically assess how policies related to eyewitness identification, forensic practices, and interrogation methods impact the accuracy of the criminal justice system. This work demonstrates my ability to evaluate academic research, interpret qualitative and quantitative data, and apply analytical thinking to real-world legal issues, highlighting the importance of reform in preventing miscarriages of justice.
